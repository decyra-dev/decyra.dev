About Decyra

Decyra was founded on a simple belief — AI is not just a technology shift, it is a shift in how work gets done and how decisions are made.

While most teams focus on tools, platforms and implementation, Decyra focuses on identifying the right problems, designing AI-assisted decision systems, and building architectures that deliver real business outcomes.

This approach moves beyond traditional data platforms and towards AI-native systems—where data, models and human workflows come together to create measurable value.

Who We Work With

Decyra partners with startups and growing organizations that are building data platforms, scaling analytics, or exploring AI initiatives— and need clarity on what to build, why it matters, and how to do it right.

About the Founder

Decyra is led by Mukund Kumar Mishra, a technology architect with 20+ years of experience designing and modernizing enterprise-scale data platforms across banking, payments, manufacturing and analytics domains.

His experience spans traditional Hadoop ecosystems to modern cloud-native architectures on Azure, Databricks and Snowflake, along with building AI-driven data platforms for large enterprises.

This combination of deep technical expertise and business-focused thinking enables Decyra to bridge the gap between architecture and real-world outcomes.
