About Decyra

Decyra is an independent architecture advisory initiative focused on modern data platforms, AI systems and scalable cloud architecture.

The advisory is led by Mukund Kumar Mishra, a technology architect with more than 16 years of experience designing enterprise-scale data platforms and analytics systems.

Mukund has worked extensively across Azure, Hadoop ecosystems, Databricks and Snowflake building large-scale data platforms for enterprises across banking, payments and analytics domains.
