About

Decyra is an independent architecture advisory initiative focused on modern data platforms, AI systems and cloud-native architecture.

The advisory is led by Mukund Kumar Mishra, a technology architect with more than 16 years of experience in enterprise data platforms, cloud engineering and large-scale analytics systems.

Mukund has designed and delivered data platforms for global enterprises across banking, payments and analytics domains using technologies such as Azure, Hadoop, Databricks and Snowflake.
