AI-Driven Credit Risk Decision Platform

Industry: Banking & Financial Services

The Problem

Credit risk analysts were manually reviewing financial statements, leading to slow decision cycles and inconsistent risk evaluation.

The Approach

Designed an AI-assisted decision system combining structured financial data, unstructured documents, and LLM-based summarization.

Architecture Highlights

  • Snowflake-based governed data platform
  • RAG pipeline for financial document analysis
  • LLM-driven risk summarization
  • Human-in-loop validation workflow

Outcome

  • 60–70% faster credit decision cycles
  • Improved consistency in risk assessment
  • Scalable underwriting operations
