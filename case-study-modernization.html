01. The Challenge

The Rigidity of Legacy Systems

The client was hindered by a legacy data warehouse that failed to scale with exponential data growth. Performance bottlenecks and rigid schema requirements created an "insights gap," where business decision-makers were operating on stale information.

High maintenance costs and a lack of support for unstructured data formats made the platform a liability for emerging AI initiatives.

02. The Architecture

Unified Data Fabric

We engineered a modern Lakehouse architecture that decouples storage from compute, enabling seamless scaling and cross-platform governance.

Multi-Engine Interop

Leveraging Snowflake for high-concurrency BI and Microsoft Fabric (OneLake) for unified, engine-agnostic data access.

Medallion Design

Implemented a tiered data quality framework (Bronze/Silver/Gold) to ensure data lineage and trust at every stage.

AI-Native ELT

Optimized pipelines designed to feed both traditional reporting and vector-based AI workloads simultaneously.
