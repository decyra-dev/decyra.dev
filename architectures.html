Architecture Patterns for Data & AI Systems

These are not generic reference diagrams. They represent proven architecture patterns used to solve real business problems—designed to support scalable data platforms, AI workloads and decision systems.

Each pattern can be adapted based on your context, scale and business goals.

Most architecture diagrams show components. We focus on how those components enable better decisions, faster execution, and real business outcomes.

Modern Data Platform

A scalable data platform architecture designed for reliable ingestion, transformation and analytics—forming the foundation for reporting, insights and downstream AI use cases.

AI-Native Data Platform

An evolution of traditional platforms to support AI workloads, unstructured data, and decision-oriented systems using modern cloud-native architecture patterns.

Lakehouse Architecture

Unified data architecture enabling data engineering, analytics and machine learning on a single platform for faster iteration and reduced complexity.

Cloud Data Warehouse Architecture

Scalable analytics architecture designed for high-performance querying, data sharing and enterprise reporting workloads.

From Patterns to Real Systems

These patterns are starting points—not final solutions. Every organization has unique constraints, data landscapes and business goals.

Decyra works with you to adapt these patterns into architectures that align with your decision workflows, scale requirements and long-term strategy.
