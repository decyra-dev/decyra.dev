Case Study: Enterprise AI

AI Enterprise Assistant

RAG + Multi-Agent Architecture for Enterprise Knowledge & Decision Support

01. The Challenge

Fragmented Intelligence

Enterprise knowledge was distributed across siloed data platforms, static documents, and legacy tools. Users relied on manual search, leading to decision-making bottlenecks.

02. The Solution

AI-Native Orchestration

Data Integration

Unified structured and unstructured flows into a single source of truth.

Contextual RAG

Vector-based retrieval ensures the LLM has the exact "fact-sheet" it needs.

Multi-Agent Logic

Independent agents for reasoning, task execution, and validation.

Instant

Access to contextual knowledge

Zero
